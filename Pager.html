A Unified Pager & Portophone Solution for FCA

FF-NL-FCA Utrecht

The Goal: A modern, software-based solution for critical alerts and communication

We must replace our outdated hardware pager system and standardize on our new mobile platform.

The Challenge

  • Hardware pager system for PLC errors is outdated.
  • Legacy Zebra devices are slow and being phased out.

The Desired State

  • **Modernize:** Critical PLC alerts on mobile devices.
  • **Unify:** Portophone functionality for all captains.
  • **Standardize:** Solution runs on the Honeywell CT45xp.

This Narrows Our Choice to Two Viable Strategies

The core decision is between a unified platform and a strategic partnership suite.

Option A: The Unified Platform

One application to manage both alerts and voice communication.

+ Simplicity & single vendor

- Higher cost & a compromise on PTT

Option B: The Strategic Suite

A best-in-class alerting app paired with our hardware partner's PTT solution.

+ Superior PTT & leverages partnership

- Two apps to manage

Proposal: Implement the Strategic Suite Solution

We recommend moving forward with Option B: SIGNL4 for alerting and Honeywell Smart Talk for PTT.

Why this option?

  • Delivers best-in-class performance for both functions.
  • Most cost-effective solution.
  • Strengthens our strategic partnership with Honeywell.

Immediate Next Steps

  • Gain stakeholder approval for the solution.
  • Initiate procurement for SIGNL4 licenses.
  • Engage Honeywell to finalize Smart Talk software licenses.
  • Schedule rollout and training with the FCA team.
